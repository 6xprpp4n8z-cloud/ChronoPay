# Privacy Policy for ChronoPay

**Effective date:** 3/1/2026  
**Developer:** John Steinmetz   
**Support contact:** jsteinmetz096@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how ChronoPay (the “App”) handles information.

## Summary
- We do not run our own servers for storing your bill data.
- We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data for advertising, marketing, analytics, or tracking.
- Your data is stored on your device and, if you enable iCloud, synced using Apple iCloud (CloudKit).

## Information you enter in the App
ChronoPay allows you to create and manage bill information that you choose to enter, such as:
- bill names and details
- amounts
- due dates
- categories
- notes and reminders
- other information you choose to store in the App

This information is used only to provide the App’s features.

## Storage and syncing (Local device storage and iCloud / CloudKit)
### Local device storage
If iCloud is not enabled or not available, your data is stored locally on your device using iOS storage.

### iCloud / CloudKit
If you enable iCloud for ChronoPay, the App uses Apple iCloud (CloudKit) to store and sync your data across your devices and to support shared household access (if you use that feature).

Apple’s handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policy. See:
- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- iCloud Legal / Privacy: https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/icloud/

We do not receive your bill data on developer-controlled servers.

## Household sharing
ChronoPay may allow you to share a household with other users (for example, by inviting another Apple ID to access the same household data).

If you enable household sharing:
- The data in that household will be accessible to the people you invite and who accept.
- Household members may be able to view or edit shared data depending on the permissions and design of the App.
- You can remove a household member to stop further access.

Important: removing a member prevents future syncing and access, but it may not delete data already stored on that member’s devices.

## Data we collect
We do not collect personal information from your use of ChronoPay such as:
- advertising identifiers
- analytics or usage profiling
- location data
- contact lists
- device data for tracking

ChronoPay does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

## Support communications
If you contact us (for example by email), we will receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address and the contents of your message. We use this information only to respond to you and provide support.

## Sharing with third parties
We do not share your in-app data with third parties, except:
- Apple iCloud / CloudKit when you enable iCloud syncing and sharing features

If we add any third-party services in the future (such as crash reporting or analytics), we will update this policy before doing so.

## Data retention and deletion
Your data is stored until you delete it.
You can delete individual items or clear your data from within the App.

If iCloud sync is enabled, deletions are synced through iCloud. Apple may retain certain information according to its own policies (for example, iCloud backups).

## Security
We use Apple platform security features and iCloud / CloudKit (if enabled) to store and sync data. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

## Children’s privacy
ChronoPay is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
